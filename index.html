Hi, I'm Jane!

I'm a software engineer that's unconventially found their way into tech by looking for new opportunities to grow, learn and innovate with all the skills I have.

My journey into tech wasn't straightforward. For a few years, I'd been working in Social Media Marketing to try something new in my first out-of-uni job.

Funnily enough, I was inspired to somehow work in tech after attending Web Summit in Lisbon a few years ago while working in Social Media, and decided to work for tech companies in Customer Support to get into tech. I took the plunge to self-fund a bootcamp course. After 6 months of pushing myself to work on progressively tougher projects, I managed to complete a course which has equipped me with the necessary skills to take on new challenges in my next role.

For me, engineering bridges the gap between the parts of me that allow me to pick up things quickly and problem-solving, with the parts of me that are creative and eager to find solutions that are impactful to people's lives.

My goal as a junior engineer is to work collaboratively with talented people across all parts of a business to gain valuable insights, refine my skills, and contribute meaningfully to projects that help people.

At the moment, I'm drawn to projects where I could be working on the backend, but I'm open to develop my skills in other avenues.

The next few years of working as an engineer look exciting! I'll be looking at contributing in open source projects and working in hackathons. If you would like to work with me on some projects, please feel free to reach out!
